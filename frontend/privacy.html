guessPop - Privacy Policy

Introduction

guessPop values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application within the Pi Network ecosystem.

What We(may) Collect

  • Basic Pi account information (username) for authentication.
  • Gameplay data such as high scores, streaks, and achievements.
  • No sensitive financial or personal data is collected.

How We Use Your Data

  • To track your progress and display leaderboards.
  • To improve game features and user experience.
  • To ensure fair play within the guessPop community.
⚠️ Sometimes, answers may be marked wrong due to data or content errors. If you believe your answer was correct, please contact us at cococake7100@gmail.com. We will review and make corrections as needed.

We comply with Pi Network’s principles of fairness, transparency, and user ownership.
